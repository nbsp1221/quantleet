# Beta Scope

## Supported

The first public beta supports:

- single-symbol historical OHLCV backtesting
- single-timeframe strategy runs
- long-or-flat research workflows
- in-memory, CSV-backed, and exchange-backed historical data loading
- market, limit, stop-market, and stop-limit orders
- fixed quantity and `qty_percent` sizing
- conservative reservation, fills, positions, reports, and plots
- finite grid parameter exploration

## Unsupported

The first public beta does not support:

- live trading
- paper trading
- shorting
- leverage or margin
- multi-symbol portfolios
- multi-timeframe strategies
- optimizer guarantees
- trading recommendation claims

Unsupported items may be planned later, but they are not current beta behavior.
