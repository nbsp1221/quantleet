not a workflow manual.

  governing design document for the task.
- Use [`../ARCHITECTURE.md`](../ARCHITECTURE.md) for the top-level domain map,
  dependency rules, and safety tiers.

  [`design-docs/quantcraft-architecture.md`](design-docs/quantcraft-architecture.md)
  for the long-lived architecture contract.
- Use
  [`design-docs/architecture-governance.md`](design-docs/architecture-governance.md)
  as background for how docs graduate into narrow mechanical checks. It is not
  a second workflow authority.
- Product behavior and shipped scope belong under
  [`product-specs/`](product-specs/), not in this design entry doc.
- Workflow mechanics live in [`../AGENTS.md`](../AGENTS.md) and the active plan
  under [`plans/`](plans/).
- The Compound Engineering migration remains an active experiment in
  [`plans/2026-04-13-ce-workflow-migration-plan.md`](plans/2026-04-13-ce-workflow-migration-plan.md);
  it is not the standing repo contract.
